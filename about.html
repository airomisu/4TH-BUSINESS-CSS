Our Story

Tangy Delights started with a simple passion: to share the joy of perfectly crystallized fruit. What began as a small kitchen experiment has grown into a beloved local treat shop.

Our Mission

To provide the freshest, crunchiest, and most delightful Tanghulu experience, using only premium fruits and high-quality ingredients.

Why We Are Unique

  • Hand-picked fresh fruits daily
  • Thin, glass-like sugar coating (never sticky!)
  • Creative flavor combinations
  • Eco-friendly packaging

Who Is This For?

For the sweet tooths, the fruit lovers, and anyone looking for that satisfying CRUNCH! Whether you're a student needing a snack or a foodie looking for the next trend, Tangy Delights is for you.
